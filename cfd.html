Call for Demos

Please download the CNSM CfD flyer here:

Important Dates

Demo Paper Submission: 20 August 2022 (extended, firm)
Demo Acceptance Notification: 12 September 2022
Camera Ready Due : 16 September 2022

The 18th International Conference on Network and Service Management (CNSM 2022) calls for demonstration proposals on research prototypes, covering all aspects of the management of networks and services, pervasive systems, enterprises, and cloud computing environments. Demonstrations showcasing new working systems, innovative applications, ground-breaking ideas, and novel concepts related to network and service management are most welcome.

Types of Demos

The expected types of contributions include but are not limited to :

  • Demos from faculty and academic research groups related to CNSM areas
  • Demos from industrial researchers
  • Demos from authors about the concepts presented in their paper(s)

A best demo award will be presented at the CNSM 2022 closing ceremony.

Submission Guidelines

Prospective authors are invited to submit demo proposals, preferably incorporating a demo system architecture illustration, using the IEEE 2-column conference format, and are limited to 3 pages including references. The proposal should also describe what the CNSM 2022 audience will experience during the demonstration. The proposal should provide the demo background and further focus on the demo settings and the application, possibly with visual outcomes to illustrate the scope of the demo.

Demo papers must be submitted electronically through EDAS in PDF format only.

Demo papers will be peer-reviewed and accepted demo papers will be included in the digital conference proceedings provided to CNSM 2022 attendees.

At least one author of each accepted demo paper proposal is required to register to CNSM 2022 conference and present the demo at the scheduled demo session.

Please feel free to contact the Demo Co-Chairs should you have any questions regarding the demo content or setup.
