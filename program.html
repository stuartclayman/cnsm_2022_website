CNSM 2021 Program

Here is the program for CNSM 2021. All times are for the CEST timezone.

The Proceedings of CNSM 2021 are available here: http://dl.ifip.org/db/conf/cnsm/cnsm2021/index.html

 

You can also see the paper selection for each session of the program.

  • Technical Sessions
    Each paper will be allocated 30 minutes.
  • Mini Conference
    Each paper will be allocated 20 minutes.
  • Posters
    Each paper will be allocated 10 minutes.
  • Demo Sessions
    Each demo will be allocated 15 minutes.
  • Workshop Papers
    Each workshop paper will be allocated 30 minutes.
