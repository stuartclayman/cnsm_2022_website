Student Travel Grant Information for CNSM 2022

A limited number of Student Travel Grants (STG) will be awarded by IFIP and CNSM 2022 (750 Euro each).
Application deadline: 20 September 2022

Eligibility Criteria

  • The applicant must be the author or co‚Äêauthor of a full, mini-conference, or short paper accepted for publication at CNSM 2022 or its co-located workshops.
  • The applicant must be a full-time student registered toward a Bachelors, Masters, or Ph.D. degree in computer science, engineering or related field in a college or university when submitting his/her application.
  • The applicant must have registered for the conference at the time of application and must attend the event to present the paper.

Application Procedure

The applicant should send an application containing:

  • name, contact information, and country;
  • institution, academic program (B.Sc., M.Sc or Ph.D.) he/she is currently attending;
  • paper ID, title and type (paper/poster/application);
  • a letter that briefly summarizes the applicant's current research interests and accomplishments;
  • a supporting letter from the advisor and his/her contact information.

Applications should be submitted by September 20, 2022 via e-mail to the Student Travel Grant Co-Chairs Alberto Schaeffer-Filho (alberto@inf.ufrgs.br) and Jin Zhao (jzhao@fudan.edu.cn).
The email subject has to be CNSM 2022 STG application. The Student Travel Grant Committee will notify applicants about award decisions starting from 30 September.
