VISA Information

Foreign participants entering Greece must hold a valid passport and may need a VISA. Further information about VISA requirements is available at: https://www.mfa.gr/en/visas/visas-for-foreigners-traveling-to-greece/countries-requiring-or-not-requiring-visa.html

An invitation letter to CNSM 2022 will be issued to:

  • authors, keynote speakers, presenters, panelists, and organizing committee members
  • conference participants who have paid their registration fee

If you need an invitation letter to apply for a VISA, please send the following information to the Local Arrangement Chair (kalafatidis@uom.edu.gr):

  • Title (Ms./Mr./Dr./Prof.)
  • Name
  • Male/Female
  • Organization
  • Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Citizenship
  • Passport No.
  • Paper Title (if any)

Please keep in mind that VISA arrangements are the responsibility of attendees and may take time to be completed.
